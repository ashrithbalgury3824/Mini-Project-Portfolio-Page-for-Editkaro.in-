Frequently Asked Questions

What types of video editing services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of video editing services including gaming videos, football edits, documentary-style videos, eCommerce ads, anime videos, short-form content, and color grading.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing depends on the complexity, length, and type of video editing required. Please contact us with your specific requirements for a custom quote.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies based on the project complexity and our current workload. Simple edits may be completed within 24-48 hours, while more complex projects might take 3-7 days.

Do you offer revisions on completed work?

Yes, we include 2-3 revision rounds in our standard packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product.

What file formats do you work with?

We work with all standard video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and WEBM. We can deliver the final product in your preferred format.
